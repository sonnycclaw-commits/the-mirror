# Value Proposition: S.T.A.R.S.

**Created:** 2026-01-14
**Status:** Distilled from 173k words of ideation + adversarial review

---

## The One Sentence

**ChatGPT remembers what you said. This tracks what you did — and predicts where you'll fail before you do.**

---

## The Moat

The claimed moat is **structured behavioral accumulation**:

| What | ChatGPT | S.T.A.R.S. |
|------|---------|------------|
| Remembers goals | Yes | Yes |
| Tracks if you followed through | No | Yes |
| Detects said/did contradiction | No | Yes |
| Predicts vulnerability windows | No | Yes (claimed) |

**The honest assessment:**

This moat requires behavioral data integration (calendar, health, screen time) — which is:
1. High-friction onboarding (permissions, trust)
2. Easily copied once proven valuable
3. Unvalidated (no evidence users want this tracked)

**Current moat status:** Theoretical. Requires 3-6 months of user behavior data before predictions become meaningful. No stickiness until then.

---

## The Proof Point (10 Minutes)

Show a user their **Delta** after 2 weeks of use:

> "You said 'family first' on Day 1. In the past 14 days:
> - 4 calendar events with family (12 hours)
> - 38 work meetings (76 hours)
> - Screen time: 6.2 hrs/day average
>
> Your stated priority does not match your behavioral reality.
> Here's what typically precedes your overwork patterns..."

If this creates an "oh shit" moment ChatGPT cannot, the moat exists.

---

## The Elevator Pitch (30 Seconds)

"Life coaches cost $300/hour. ChatGPT gives generic advice. Neither tracks whether you actually followed through.

We're building the first AI that knows the difference between what you say and what you do — and intervenes before you slip, not after.

Think of it as a 'check engine light' for your life, trained on your actual behavior."

---

## Honest Assessment

### Is This Differentiated Enough?

**Short answer: Not yet.**

The differentiation depends on:
1. **Behavioral tracking** — requires device integrations users may resist
2. **Prediction accuracy** — unproven; needs 3+ months of data per user
3. **Intervention effectiveness** — the "Walk" (action layer) is completely unspecified

From CHALLENGES.md: *"The core bet is on personalized pathways and behavioral transformation. Neither is designed."*

### What Would Make It Defensible?

| Moat Type | Requirement | Timeline |
|-----------|-------------|----------|
| Data network effects | Cross-user pattern learning | 12+ months, 10k+ users |
| Validated accuracy | Published research showing prediction works | 6-12 months |
| Behavior change science | Partnership with researchers/therapists | 6+ months |
| Integration lock-in | Deep Apple Health / Google Fit integration | 3+ months dev |

**None of these exist today.**

### The Kill Question

> "What can you show in 30 days that ChatGPT + journaling cannot?"

If the answer is "nothing yet" — the moat is aspirational, not real.

---

## Recommendation

**Do not build the full vision.**

Instead, validate the atomic hypothesis: *Can behavioral tracking + said/did contradiction create value ChatGPT cannot?*

Test with:
1. Manual "commitment logging" (no integrations)
2. Weekly follow-up: "Did you do what you said?"
3. Show the Delta after 2 weeks
4. Measure: Does this create retention/insight ChatGPT cannot?

If yes: Build the integrations.
If no: The moat doesn't exist. Pivot.
