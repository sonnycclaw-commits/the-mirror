## The Star Types

**☆ BRIGHT STAR** — Verified strength
A gold center with warm coral glow. This is something true about you that you've proven through action. It shines.

**✧ DIM STAR** — Shadow aspect
Muted gray-blue, barely visible. Not shameful — inviting. This is the part of you asking to be seen, not hidden.

**✦ FLICKERING STAR** — Emerging potential
Sage green with gold hints, unsteady glow. Something is forming here. You're not sure what yet. Neither are we.

**● DARK STAR** — Gravity well
Deep purple-black, subtle pull. This is a pattern dragging you away from where you want to go. Not evil — just honest.

## The Journey

### Days 1-7: The Mirror

Seven days of excavation. Not a quiz — a descent.

Each day, TARS guides you deeper. You answer questions that most people spend their lives avoiding:

- What dissatisfaction have you learned to tolerate?
- What would someone conclude about you by watching your behavior?
- What is the life you're terrified of living?

With each answer, a star appears. By Day 7, your **Birth Chart** reveals itself — the initial constellation of who you are right now.

The reveal isn't a moment of judgment. It's a moment of recognition.

*"Oh. There I am."*

### Days 8+: The Walk

Now you move.

Each day, your constellation shifts based on what you do, not just what you say. Make a commitment, keep it — a connection brightens. Avoid something again — a dark star pulls stronger.

TARS doesn't lecture. TARS notices.

> "You said family was your north star. But I notice all your bright stars are clustering around Work. That's not wrong — it's interesting. What do you think is happening?"

## TARS

TARS is not a chatbot. TARS is a companion.

TARS has a voice — calm, curious, occasionally wry. TARS doesn't pretend to be human, but TARS isn't cold either. TARS is the observer who sees your patterns without judgment, who asks the questions you've been avoiding, who celebrates your bright stars and sits with you in front of your dark ones.

TARS evolves too. As your constellation forms, TARS learns how to speak to *you* specifically.
